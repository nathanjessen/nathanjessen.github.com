Heylo! I am Nathan Jessen, a Front-end Architect based in Las Vegas. I love minimal design, clean code, and high performance web sites. Specialized in CSS development, I always try to enhance the developer experience through Styleguide driven development and well-architected code bases. I also try to enhance the user experience using smooth animations, simple navigation, and beautiful designs. I'm continually learning new things and enjoy speaking to/with others who are also passionate of their craft.

  2. Passion for problem-solving and developing new ways to approach an issue

  4. Curiosity when code doesn't work. Willingness to digging to the root of an issue to fix it rather than piling on temporary hacks
  5. Comfortable asking superiors, clients, or peers for additional information to ensure expectations and requirements are understood
  6. Willing to experiment with new technologies and approaching a project or task in a new way
  7. Empathetic to the client and user of a website. I will constantly ask myself if what I'm building is valuable to the client and its users or if something needs altered to better meet their needs and expectations
  8. Able to switch gears when the scope of a project changes unexpectedly or time sensitive issues arise
  9. Value my own set of problem-solving abilities and best practices instead of always following set rules and practices that may be out of date
  10. High level of communication after several years working remote
